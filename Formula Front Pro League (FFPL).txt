FORMULA FRONT PRO LEAGUE (FFPL)
================================

Formula Front Pro League is a competitive league platform built around Armored
Core: Formula Front for the PSP. Players design and tune autonomous AC units,
then submit them to battle head-to-head in fully simulated AI-driven matches —
no reflexes required, just engineering.

WHAT IT IS
----------
FFPL is a structured tournament system where your Armored Core fights for you.
Upload your AC build, enter a challenge, and watch the matchup play out through
the game's own AI combat engine. Wins and losses update your pilot rating via a
live Glicko-2 ranking system.

HOW IT WORKS
------------
1. Build your AC in-game using standard Formula Front rules.
2. Export your save file and submit it to the league via the FFPL app.
3. The league validates your loadout, registers your pilot profile, and queues
   your challenges.
4. Matches run automatically — results, stats, and updated ratings are posted
   in real time.

THE PLATFORM
------------
- Wails-based desktop client (Windows/Linux) for save-file management and
  match tracking
- Self-hosted PocketBase backend for pilot profiles, match history, and
  challenge queues
- Open-source save-file parser (github.com/AERProductions/ffpl/parser) for
  community tooling
- AGPL-3.0 licensed — any hosted fork must publish its modifications
